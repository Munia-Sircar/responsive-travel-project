Why Akhiabara?

The Electric Town of Tokyo

Akihabara is a buzzing shopping hub famed for its electronics retailers, ranging from tiny stalls to vast department stores like Yodobashi Multimedia Akiba. Venues specializing in manga, anime, and video games include Tokyo Anime Center, for exhibits and souvenirs, and Radio Kaikan with 10 floors of toys, trading cards, and collectibles. Staff dressed as maids or butlers serve tea and desserts at nearby maid cafes.

Hot Spots

Famous Attraction of Akiba

ACOS Store

Akihabara is the center of otaku culture in Japan and a must-visit spot for anime and manga fans. You can fully enjoy this culture at stores such as ACOS, which offers everything needed for cosplay. You can find costumes of your favorite characters, colorful wigs, specialized makeup, and even colored contact lenses, all necessary items for transformation.

Address

1-7-6 Sotokanda, Chiyoda-ku

Learn More

Super Potato Store

If you grew up playing the Super Famicom, Mega Drive, or the original PlayStation, Super Potato is an essential place to visit. This famous store specializes in retro games and is filled with nostalgic titles. The walls are lined with classic games from childhood, and you can find original game consoles, rare collector's items, figures.

Address

1-11-2 Sotokanda, Chiyoda-ku

Learn More

Mogra Nightclub

If you want to experience the nightlife in Akihabara, the unique nightclub Mogra is recommended for enjoying otaku culture. DJs play anime songs and music popular among otaku all night long. It is a warm and welcoming place where anime fans from around the world gather and enjoy together. Blast into the world of anime through music.

Address

B1F, Matsubishi Building, 3-11 Akihabara, Taito-ku
